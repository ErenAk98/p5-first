Random Spurs of Poetry



Hot and melting, blistering ice
Jeweled, prized a gift in size
Verily tired is this vice
Tell me of this merciless rise


Un
Crowding crawling cawing fright
Kneeling, reeling, feeling might
Thunder through my senses
Biting, eating


Know thy enemy, one said
Dire is the end of the unhearing
Temper your blade with fear
Knives cut deeper unblooded



A Knife
